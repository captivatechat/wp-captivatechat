== Description ==

Captivate Chat is a cutting-edge software-as-a-service (SaaS) application that is designed to revolutionize the way businesses connect with their customers. This innovative platform seamlessly integrates all of your omnichannels with popular live chat platforms like Microsoft Teams and Slack, allowing you to communicate with your customers in real-time, no matter where they are located.

With Captivate Chat, you can easily manage all of your customer interactions from a single, user-friendly interface. Whether your customers are reaching out to you via email, social media, or live chat, you can quickly and efficiently respond to their inquiries and provide the support they need to stay satisfied.

One of the key benefits of Captivate Chat is its ability to streamline your workflow and reduce response times. With all of your customer interactions organized in one place, you can quickly identify and prioritize incoming messages, ensuring that urgent inquiries receive prompt attention. And with real-time notifications and alerts, you'll never miss an important message or opportunity to engage with your customers.

Another advantage of using Captivate Chat is its flexibility and customization options. Whether you're a small business owner or a large corporation, you can tailor the platform to meet your specific needs and preferences. From customizing the interface to integrating with third-party applications, Captivate Chat offers a wide range of features and capabilities that can be customized to suit your unique business requirements.

In addition to its advanced features and flexibility, Captivate Chat is also incredibly user-friendly and easy to use. The platform is designed to be intuitive and straightforward, with a minimal learning curve, making it accessible to users of all skill levels.

Overall, if you're looking for a powerful, versatile, and user-friendly platform to connect your omnichannels to popular live chat platforms like Microsoft Teams and Slack, Captivate Chat is the perfect solution for you. So why wait? [Sign up now](https://hub.captivatechat.com/user/signup) today and start streamlining your customer interactions like never before!

== Installation ==

1. Upload `captivatechat` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Configure plugin (go to the Settings/Captivate Chat Plugin for WordPress)
